PRODUCTS

Oway products are rich in organic, biodynamic ingredients. Rosemary, lemon balm, sage, thyme, mint and helichrysum are grown on Ortofficina, a bio-dynamic farm in Bologna, and processed without using chemicals or solvents. Stylish and recyclable packaging completes this exceptional hair care offering from a brand that partners with fair trade networks to deliver quality, consistency and transparency.

Biodynamic, What Does It Mean?

Shortly before opening, The Barbery had the pleasure of receiving product training from Oway, the organic biodynamic and near-vegan (two of their products contain honey) haircare range we are collaborating with to treat our clients. I thought I would share with you what we learnt about how Oway grow the ingredients we find in their products.
